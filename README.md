# Privacy Policy – CBEU HRMS Mobile Application

**Effective Date:** September 08, 2025  
**Last Updated:** September 08, 2025  

CBEU HRMS (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the **CBEU HRMS Mobile Application** (“App”).  
This Privacy Policy describes how employee information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the App.

---

## 1. Overview
The CBEU HRMS Mobile Application is designed to support Human Resource operations of the organization, including attendance, leave, payroll, and employee self-service functions.  
Data collected through the App is used strictly for official HR and organizational purposes.

---

## 2. Information We Collect
We may collect and process the following categories of information:

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Employee full name  
- Employee number / ID  
- Email address  
- Contact number  
- Designation and department  
- Bank and payroll-related details (as provided by the employer)

### 2.2 Employment & Payroll Information
- Salary details  
- Allowances and deductions  
- Payslip and payroll records  

### 2.3 Attendance & Leave Information
- Clock-in / clock-out records  
- GPS location (only if enabled and required by the organization)  
- Leave applications and approval status  

### 2.4 Device & Technical Information
- Device model and operating system  
- IP address  
- Application usage logs  

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information
Collected information is used for the following purposes:
- Managing attendance, leave, and payroll processes  
- Enabling secure authentication and role-based access control  
- Delivering payslips, approvals, and HR-related notifications  
- Improving application performance and user experience  
- Ensuring compliance with internal policies and applicable laws  

---

## 4. Data Sharing & Disclosure
CBEU HRMS does **not** sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Data may be shared only with:
- **CBEU Human Resources / Authorized Officers** for official HR operations  
- **Authorized Service Providers** (such as cloud hosting or notification services) strictly for system functionality  
- **Legal or Regulatory Authorities** when required by law  

---

## 5. Data Security
We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect user data:
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest  
- Access is limited to authorized users and administrators  
- Regular system monitoring and security checks are conducted  

---

## 6. Data Retention
- Employee data is retained while the employment or user account remains active  
- Upon termination or resignation, data may be archived in accordance with CBEU HR policies and legal requirements  

---

## 7. User Rights
Employees may:
- Request access to their personal data  
- Request corrections to inaccurate or outdated information  
- Request deletion of data, subject to HR, audit, and legal obligations  

All requests should be directed to the **CBEU HR Department**.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services
The App may use trusted third-party services for:
- Cloud infrastructure  
- Push notifications  
- System analytics (if applicable)  

All third-party providers are required to follow strict data protection and confidentiality standards.

---

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.  
Any changes will be communicated through the App or via official organizational communication channels.
